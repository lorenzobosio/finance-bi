<objective>
Schedule the daily ingestion cron and surface its result loudly in the app: a global "data as of {date}" freshness banner and a passive reconnect banner — the two banners defined by the UI-SPEC.

This is the visibility slice and the operational close of Phase 1 (the "1.3 Rules + scheduler" shape from D-21): after this plan, the pull runs automatically every day, and the user always sees how fresh the data is via the global "data as of {date}" banner (D-15) and is loudly prompted to reconnect when consent lapses via a passive, non-blocking banner (D-16) — never a silent freeze. It makes ING-06 ("every dashboard shows a freshness banner") and the visible half of ING-05 ("reconnect needed" state) true.

Purpose: PSD2 has no webhooks; the freshness banner IS the dead-man's-switch detector for the MVP (proactive alerts are Phase 7). The cron is also the Supabase keep-alive. Banners are a read-plane concern — they read connections under RLS via @supabase/ssr, never the service_role client.
